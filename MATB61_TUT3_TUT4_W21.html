Welcome to the homepage for MATB61 tutorial sections TUT0003 and TUT0004 at the University of Toronto Scarborough, winter 2021. Tutorial notes and sample quiz solutions will be posted here. Important announcements and the zoom link will be posted on the Quercus page. The course description from the UTSC calendar can be found here. Please let me know if you have any suggestions or feedbacks. The main purpose of the tutorial is to help you understand the concepts that are taught in lectures by briefly going over the concepts again and doing practice problems. The four-phased method introduced by G.Polya in his book How to Solve It at 1945 will be applied when teaching. More information about the method and myself can be found in the home page.
How to academically succeed in this course:
Understand the concepts and algorithms by proving not just seeing. Do the practice problem even though some of them are relatively easy.
