MATH1510 Fundamentals of Mathematics, tutorial

TA: Nick (Zhiyong) Huang

Welcome to the homepage for MATH1510 tutorial section C at the York University, fall 2021 to winter 2022. Tutorial notes will be posted here. The main purpose of the tutorial is to help you understand the concepts that are taught in lectures by briefly going over the concepts again and doing practice problems.

The weekly tutorial is 1.5 hours and the format of the tutorial is questions-answers. We will discuss a selection of questions from the textbook in the first hour, and have office hour for any questions that you have in the remaining time. Feel free to contact me by email at nickh97@yorku.ca if you have any concerns and questions.
